Banana Bread Overnight Oats

This is by far my favorite overnight oats. There's something about a bananas in the morning that really gives me the kickstart in the morning.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Ripe Banana
  • Maple Syrup
  • Cinnamon
  • Pecans
  • Sea Salt

How To Make Banana Bread Overnight Oats

  1. Mash the banana in a small bowl or jar that can be seale
  2. Add in all other ingredients with the banana.
  3. Stir up the banana overnight oats, cover and chill for at least 4 hours.
    4. And boom, you made yourself Banana Bread Overnight Oats!
